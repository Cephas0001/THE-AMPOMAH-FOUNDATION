Our Identity

The Ampomah Group Foundation logo represents our commitment to compassion, service, and dignity. It symbolizes the hope we bring to vulnerable communities across Ghana through our humanitarian work.

Just as our logo stands strong and recognizable, our foundation stands as a beacon of hope for those in need, providing practical assistance and restoring dignity to the lives we touch.

Our Story

Ampomah Group Foundation was created to respond to the needs of the vulnerable in Ghana through compassion, service, and practical assistance. We believe that every human life is valuable and deserves care, respect, and opportunity.

Many individuals and children in Ghana face daily challenges such as lack of food, access to healthcare, shelter, and basic support. Our foundation exists to bring hope, care, and dignity to people who are struggling due to poverty, illness, abandonment, or difficult living conditions.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To create a compassionate society where the poor, the sick, orphans, and socially disadvantaged people are cared for, supported, and given hope for a better future.

Our Mission

To serve vulnerable individuals and communities in Ghana by providing humanitarian assistance, medical support, care for orphans and street children, and community outreach that restores dignity and hope.

Who We Serve

We focus our efforts on helping the most vulnerable members of society

The Poor & Underprivileged

Providing food, shelter, and basic necessities to those living in poverty.

The Sick & Medically Vulnerable

Offering medical assistance, healthcare access, and support for those facing illness.

Orphans & Orphanages

Caring for children without parents and supporting orphanages with resources.

Street Children

Reaching out to children living on the streets with care, education, and rehabilitation.
